How to get psychubes
Reverse: 1999 has no psychube gacha — every psychube comes from farming, shops, or events. The three sources you'll see tagged on each card:
Shop Standard 5★ — buy anytime in the Fragment Shop with Thought Element / Thoughts in Entirety farmed from Pneuma Analysis stages. Sig The character's signature — debuts in that character's event shop (limited), later reappears in reruns / the permanent psychube pool. The hardest to get. Event A limited 6★ from a past event shop or the Roar Jukebox battle pass — watch for reruns.
Upgrade with Engraving (levels, from dupes/mats) and Amplify 1→5 (needs duplicates; 5★/6★ only). Availability rotates — verify the live in-game shops.
